On our way to Fremont Indian State Park, it was a pretty drive.
Cool rocks to look at as we got close to the visitors center
We started out here and watched a video about the area, walked around the 
gift shop and museum.
Then we went outside for a short walk around the center. There are many trails to hike in the area but because of time limits we just did one. We were given a free map to follow the signs on the trails and read about different rock art.
 

  

Checking out the pit house
